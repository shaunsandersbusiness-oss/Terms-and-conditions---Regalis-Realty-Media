Where a client-provided script causes the finished video to exceed the standard 75-second runtime, a length overage fee of $75 per additional 30 seconds, or any portion thereof, will apply. By way of example, a finished runtime of 135 seconds is two 30-second increments beyond the 75-second standard, for a length overage fee of $150. When the script is submitted on time (see 6c) and the pre-production call is completed (see 6d), this fee is quoted and approved before the shoot.
