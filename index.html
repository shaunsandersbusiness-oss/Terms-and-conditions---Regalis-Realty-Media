PRICING MODEL — PRICED BY HOME SIZE

Regalis Realty Media prices most listing Services by home square footage, across defined size tiers, rather than by photo count. The number of final images varies with the size, layout, and features of the property; a typical home receives approximately 25–50 finished images. No fee is charged for delivering more or fewer images than a typical set.

New Jersey & the boroughs and Manhattan are priced as distinct markets; the applicable market is determined by the property's location.

05

SQUARE FOOTAGE, PROPERTY SIZE & PRICE DETERMINATION

Because price is determined by home size, accurate square footage is essential. The following governs how square footage is established and how discrepancies are handled.

(a) Client declaration. At booking, the client provides the property's square footage and selects (or is quoted) the corresponding size tier. The client represents that this figure is accurate to the best of their knowledge.

(b) Scan-measured square footage controls. For any booking that includes a floor-plan scan or 3D scan (2D floor plan, 3D floor plan, or Zillow 3D tour), the gross living area measured by that scan is the authoritative square footage and determines the applicable price tier — regardless of the figure declared at booking. If the measured size falls within a higher tier than the one booked, the price is adjusted up to the correct tier.

(c) Photo-only bookings (no scan). Where no scan is performed, Regalis Realty Media may verify the property's square footage against public records (county tax/assessor records, MLS data) and on-site observation. The applicable tier is set by the greater of the declared figure or the verified figure.

(d) Correction to the proper tier — no penalty. Where the correct square footage places the property in a higher tier than booked, Regalis Realty Media simply adjusts to the correct tier. No penalty or misdeclaration fee is charged — the client pays only the correct published rate for the property's actual size.

(e) Billing the difference. If the client has not yet paid, the corrected tier is reflected on the invoice before delivery. If the client has already paid at the lower (booked) tier, Regalis Realty Media will add the difference and re-send an updated invoice for the balance due.

(f) Unpaid balances carry to the next job. If a tier-difference balance is not paid, it remains due and will be automatically applied to and added onto the client's next booking or invoice. Consistent with Section 06, deliverables remain locked until the applicable invoice is paid in full, and outstanding balances accrue late-payment interest as provided in Section 16.

(g) Evidence. Any tier adjustment is based on the scan measurement and/or public records (county tax/assessor records, MLS data), not on on-site negotiation.
